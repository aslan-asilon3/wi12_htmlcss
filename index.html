HOW TO GET TO TORREVIEJA?

Alicante Airport (ALC)


To get from Alicante Airport to Torrevieja, you can either:

  • - Get a Taxi. Price (approx.): 65£/taxi. Time: 45 min. Phone: +34 965 10 16 11
    You may take a taxi at the airport, but they might be more expensive.
  • - Get a Bus: Price: 7€. Time: 1h 20m.

Murcia Airport (RMU)


To get from Murcia Airport to Torrevieja, you can:

  • - Get a Taxi. Price (approx.): 76£/taxi. Time: 1h. Phone: +34 968 24 88 00
    There will also be taxis at the airport.
